Welcome to SpeedFlag,

The flag guessing game that will put your geographical knowledge to the test! Ready for the challenge?

How to play :

Launching the game

Click on the button to start the 30-seconds countdown.

Flags challenge

Meanwhile, look carefully at each flag on the screen. Use your knowledge of geography to identify the country corresponding to each flag.

Quick response

Type the name of the country you think matches the flag in the input box and press "Enter" to submit your answer.

Win points

Each correct answer earns you points. The more flags you guess correctly, the higher your score.

Challenge yourself

See how many flags you can guess before time runs out. Push your limits and improve your score with every game!
